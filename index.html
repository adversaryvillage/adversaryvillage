Adversary Village at DEF CON

DEF CON 33, Las Vegas Convention Center, August 7-10, 2025

DEF CON is more than just a conference, It is an ecosystem of specialized communities and villages, including the Adversary Village! Villages are dedicated, independently organized spaces focused on specific topics, functioning like mini-conferences within DEF CON Hacker Convention. Each village hosts its own talks, hands-on activities, and interactive experiences, such as CTF competitions and technical workshops for attendees to interact with the specific area of their focus.
We are excited to be back in DEF CON this year and see you all there at Las Vegas!
