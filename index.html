Adversary Villge at DEF CON 32

09-11 August, 2024. Las Vegas Convention Center in Las Vegas, Nevada.

Adversary Village shall focus on hosting hands-on deep technical workshops, live demonstrations, panel discussions, adversary wars - CTF competition and a ton of other hands-on activities on adversarial attack simulation/emulation, adversary tradecraft and offensive cyber security. See you all there at DEF CON hacker convention this year!

Adversary Village is a community initiative which purely focuses on adversary tactics, adversary simulation/emulation, threat/APT/Ransomware emulation, breach and attack simulation, supply chain security, adversary life, adversary mindset, philosophy, urban survival skills and purple teaming.
