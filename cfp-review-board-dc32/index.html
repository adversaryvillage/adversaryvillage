Meet the CFP review board for Adversary Village at DEF CON 32!


Jonathan Baker

Director, Center for Threat-Informed Defense | MITRE Engenuity

Jon Baker is the founding Director of Research and Development for MITRE Engenuity’s Center for Threat-Informed Defense. In addition to co-founding the Center, he is the Cyber Threat Intelligence and Adversary Emulation Department Manager in The MITRE Corporation. Jon has spent much of the past eighteen years at MITRE.

Anant Shrivastava

Founder and Chief Researcher, Cyfinoid Research

Anant Shrivastava is an information security professional with 12+ yrs of corporate experience. During his career has been a speaker and a trainer at various international conferences (Black Hat -USA, ASIA, EU, Nullcon, c0c0n and many more)

Bryson Bort

Founder and CEO of SCYTHE | GRIMM | Co-Founder of ICS Village

Bryson is the Founder of SCYTHE, a start-up building a next generation attack emulation platform, and GRIMM, a cybersecurity consultancy, and Co-Founder of the ICS Village, a non-profit advancing awareness of industrial control system security.
