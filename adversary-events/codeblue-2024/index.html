Choose-your-own-Adversary-Adventure Tabletop Game

Adversary Village area | November 14-15, 2024 | 09:00 to 17:00

Adversary Adventure is a Choose-Your-Own-Adventure model interactive game, where everyone can participate and choose various tasks. The participants can choose to play as an attacker who performs adversarial activities against a target, a defender who deals with a potential breach, as a CISO who is managing a ransomware attack, or even as management executives going through a table-top exercise.

Adversary Village CTF

Adversary Village area | November 14-15, 2024 | 09:00 to 17:00

Adversary Village is excited to introduce a Capture the Flag contest, where participants will have the opportunity to apply the skills they have acquired during the Day-1 workshop. This contest will present a series of challenges that will test their knowledge in adversary emulation and attacker tradecraft. There would be real-world simulation of CTF scenarios and challenges, where the participants can perform various attacks and learn new attack vectors, TTPs, techniques, etc.

Top performers in the contest will be rewarded with exciting prizes and exclusive goodies.
