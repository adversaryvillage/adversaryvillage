Talk: Phish Like An APT

Have you ever wondered what phishing strategy real world APTs use? And how these compare with the scenarios that you use during your Red Team / social engineering activities? If you did, you probably found out that there's a lot of research about APT techniques, tactics and procedures, like the use of specific malware or attack vectors, but there are not many public resources on which techniques those attackers actually use to convince a non-suspecting person to aid them in their operation. In this talk an analysis is presented of hundreds of phishing emails that were used in real campaigns.
