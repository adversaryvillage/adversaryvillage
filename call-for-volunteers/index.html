We need volunteers!
Adversary Village
at DEF CON 30


Adversary Village at DEF CON is a community initiative which purely focuses on Adversary simulation, Adversary emulation, Threat/APT emulation, Breach and adversarial attack simulation, Supply chain security, Adversary Tactics, Life, Adversary Philosophy, urban survival skills and a bit of Purple teaming.
We are looking for volunteers who can help us with Adversary Village at DEF CON 30 in-person conference, Las Vegas. You dont need to be highly skilled in Adversary simulation or offensive security. You just need to have a hacker mindset, helping mentality and ability to show up when it is required. There will be opportunities for you to learn a ton, meet Adversary tactics experts and security gurus! If you are willing to contribute, please feel free to submit the form!
