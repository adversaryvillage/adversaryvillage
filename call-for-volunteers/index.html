We need volunteers!
Adversary Village
at DEF CON 33


We need awesome volunteers to lend a hand during Adversary Village at DEF CON 33, taking place in Las Vegas! 8-10, August 2025.
You dont need to be highly skilled in Adversary simulation or offensive cyber security. You just need to have a hacker mindset, helping mentality and ability to show up when it is required. There will be opportunities for you to learn a ton, meet Adversary tactics experts and security gurus! If you are interested to contribute, please feel free to submit the form!

This is a community-driven event. We will not be able to pay volunteers or cover any travel/lodging expenses.
However, you will receive:
Adversary Village volunteer tshirt/hoodies
Love and respect from the community,
An awesome Adversary Village goodie bag,
Adversary Village badges,
Letter of Recommendation for the volunteers
and an opportunity to network with industry leaders, speakers, village organizers and a ton of participants

Note on DEF CON Passes:
We are inviting volunteers who plan to attend DEF CON 33 and are able to cover their travel and accommodation. If you already have a DEF CON 33 pass, that is awesome! Although we hope to offer conference badges to our volunteers, availability will depend on availability and cannot be guaranteed at this stage. But, we may have very limited passes, and those will be distributed on a first-come, first-served basis.
Priority will be given to students and DEF CON first-timers.
We encourage those interested to join us for the rewarding experience and to contribute to the community, with the understanding that a badge may not be assured for everyone.

Volunteer Expectations:
We appreciate your commitment and love towards the hacker community and willingness to contribute your valuable time and effort!
We are looking for only a limited number of volunteers, If selected, you will hear back from us via email or Discord.
Selection has nothing to do with your skills or expertise. We are mostly looking for; How much time you can contribute, What areas you are able to support during the event (logistics, speaker ops, moderation, etc.) (Areas where we need more help)

Code of Conduct:
By submitting the volunteer form, you agree to abide by the DEF CON code of conduct if selected.
