Adversary Wars CTF 2.0
DEF CON 30

Adversary Village CTF Area,
Flamingo, Las Vegas
12 - 14 August, 2022

Adversary Village will be hosting a small scale CTF named "Adversary Wars", where the participants will have to pose as adversaries, and simulate adversarial actions against each element of the dummy target organization.

Adversary Wars CTF will have real world simulation CTF scenarios and challenges, where the adversaries can simulate attacks and learn new attack vectors, TTPs, techniques, etc. There would be combined exercises which include different levels of threat/adversary emulation and purple teaming.

Refer adv-wars-ctf channel in DEF CON official Discord server https://discord.gg/defcon for CTF discussions and registration information!
